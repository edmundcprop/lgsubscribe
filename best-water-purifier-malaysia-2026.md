## What to look for in a water purifier (the short version)

Before comparing models, get clear on four things.

**Filtration type.** Ultra Filtration (UF) removes bacteria, viruses and heavy metals without stripping minerals. Reverse Osmosis (RO) removes almost everything, including minerals, and wastes more water. For most Malaysian municipal tap water, UF is the practical choice.

**Tankless vs tank.** Tankless purifiers filter water only when you press the button. No stagnant water sitting in a reservoir overnight. Tank models store pre-chilled or pre-heated water so they can dispense faster during peak use. Both work — but tankless is now the industry direction for hygiene.

**Installation type.** Countertop units sit on your kitchen counter and need a water line. Under-sink models hide the body below and leave only a spout visible. Floor-standing units suit offices, pantries and open kitchens where counter space is tight.

**Maintenance.** Self-service plans ship filters to your door every six months and you swap them yourself. Regular Visit plans send a technician on schedule. The right choice depends on how hands-on you want to be — not on which is "better."
